Money

Money is any medium of exchange that can be used to pay for goods and services and to measure the value of things. Currency is a term for a country's money in circulation—that is, coins and bills.

Money is finite so it is crucial to make smart decisions when it comes to spending the money that you have. Once you spend the money you have, you don’t have more to spend unless you make more.
